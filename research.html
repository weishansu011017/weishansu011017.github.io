Research

Gas and dust dynamics

I study the evolution of gas and dust in protoplanetary and circumbinary discs, especially under perturbations from stellar flybys.

Streaming instability

I investigate when disturbed dust distributions become favourable for streaming instability and dust concentration.

Scientific computing

I develop GPU-accelerated Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics (SPH) interpolation and neighbour search based on state-of-the-art Linear Bounding Volume Hierarchy (LBVH) construction methods.
