AstroPostprocess

Julia packages for astrophysical simulation post-processing and particle data analysis.

  • Partia.jl — CPU and GPU SPH interpolation, neighbour search, LBVH construction, and traversal.
  • UnsignedRadixSorts.jl — Radix sorting for UInt32 and UInt64 arrays.
  • StreamingInstability.jl — Linear streaming-instability growth-rate calculations.
  • TinyEigvals.jl — Eigenvalue calculations for small fixed-size complex matrices.
