Research

Numerical astrophysics

Hydrodynamic simulations of circumstellar discs, galactic discs, stellar feedback, and dust–gas interactions.

Particle methods

Smoothed particle hydrodynamics, spatial interpolation, neighbour searches, and linear bounding volume hierarchies.

GPU computing

CUDA and Metal implementations of particle algorithms, eigensolvers, and streaming instability growth rate calculations.

Software

I develop and maintain open-source software under AstroPostprocess , a GitHub organisation for numerical astrophysics, particle methods, data analysis, and GPU computing.

  • Partia.jl — CPU and GPU algorithms for particle neighbour searches, linear bounding volume hierarchy construction and traversal, and SPH interpolation.
  • TinyEigvals.jl — A GPU batch eigensolver for small fixed-size non-Hermitian matrices.
  • StreamingInstability.jl — Tools for calculating and analysing streaming instability growth rates.
